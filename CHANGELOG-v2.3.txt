Project Guardian v2.3 — Waypoint Patrol

Changes:
- Replaced random patrol movement with a deterministic five-point waypoint route.
- Guardian now walks back and forth through the scene and pauses naturally at each post.
- Movement is Promise-based and ends only after the CSS left-position transition completes.
- Guardian automatically switches to idle after reaching each waypoint.
- Added a movement cancellation token to prevent overlapping patrol, combat, and recall animations.
- Combat cleanly interrupts the route and resumes it after the Red Knight is defeated.
- Red Knight encounter is scripted to occur about nine seconds after awakening.
- Removed random run commands that caused the Guardian to animate in one place.
